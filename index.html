About

I am interested in Natural Language Processing research concerning LLMs, dialogue, and emotion. I love exploring algorithms related to virtual assistants, evaluation, and affective computing.

I am currently a Research Engineer (Level 3) at ASAPP exploring research relating to tool calling dialogue agents to enhance customer service.

I recieved my Masters in Computer Science and Engineering with a focus on AI from the University of Michigan in 2022. Prior to that in 2020 I recieved a B.S. in Computer Science and B.S. in Mathematics from Virginia Tech.
